\begin{center}
    \Large\textbf{About This Book}
\end{center}

\vspace{1cm}

This book offers a structured and comprehensive introduction to neural networks for students, self-learners, and practitioners in Artificial Intelligence. It begins with mathematical foundations and activation functions, then develops the core ideas of architectures, optimization, training, data processing, and modern large-scale models, with special attention to both conceptual clarity and practical implementation.

\textbf{Main Features:}
\begin{itemize}
    \item A complete 13-chapter progression from mathematical foundations to Transformers and modern training techniques
    \item Rigorous derivations, intuitive explanations, and carefully designed figures throughout the text
    \item Practical implementation examples centered on the PyTorch ecosystem
    \item Coverage of core architectures and mechanisms, including CNNs, RNNs, skip connections, and attention
    \item Dedicated chapters on optimization, training workflows, dataset processing, and computational efficiency
    \item A carefully curated bibliography that connects foundational papers with modern developments
\end{itemize}
